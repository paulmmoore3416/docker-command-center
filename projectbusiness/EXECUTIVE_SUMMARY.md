## The Opportunity

Docker Command Center addresses a critical gap in the containerization market: **simple, intuitive container management for teams of all sizes**. The global container management market is worth $12.3B (2025) and growing 25% annually, yet existing solutions are either too complex (Kubernetes), too basic (Docker Desktop), or hard to use (Portainer).

**DCC's unique value:**
- ✅ Works with Docker (no Kubernetes learning curve)
- ✅ Multi-host management from single dashboard
- ✅ Native mobile app for on-call management
- ✅ 80% faster to productivity than competitors
- ✅ 60% lower TCO than enterprise alternatives
